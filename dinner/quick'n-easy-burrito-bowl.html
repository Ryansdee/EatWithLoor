Savor the bold flavors of our Burrito Bowl, a quick and easy dish that's perfect for a satisfying weeknight meal. This recipe features seasoned minced meat of your choice, combined with black or red beans and a medley of vibrant veggies. Layered over a base of crisp iceberg lettuce and fluffy rice, it's topped with a zesty salsa and a sprinkle of grated cheese. Drizzle with your favorite taco sauce and, if desired, add a dollop of sour cream for an extra burst of creaminess. A delightful and customizable dish ready to please your taste buds in just 30 minutes!
