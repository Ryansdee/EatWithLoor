Ingredients

    - For the tomato sauce -

  • 3 large onions, halved and thinly sliced

  • 300 grams diced bacon bits

  • 500 grams of canned chopped tomatoes in rich juice

  • 250 grams of slices of lasagne (check pack for cooking instructions)

  • 1-2 tbsp of olive oil

  • Spices of choice (e.g., salt, pepper, oregano, garlic, etc.)

    • - For the creamy white sauce -

  • 600 milliliter milk

  • 50 grams butter

  • 50 grams plain flour

  • 50 grams grated parmesan cheese

  • Spices of choice (e.g., salt, pepper, nutmeg, etc.)

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan. Add onions and cook for about 15 mins until golden. Add chopped bacon, cook for another 5 mins, stirring frequently.

  2. Add the canned tomatoes, season with salt and pepper, and let it bubble uncovered for 5 mins. Remove from heat.

  3. For the white sauce, pour milk into a pan. Add butter and flour. Whisk continuously over moderate heat until thickened. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg.

  4. Spoon a third of the tomato sauce into a lasagne dish. Layer with a third of the lasagne sheets. Repeat with another third of sauce and lasagne sheets. Finish with the remaining tomato sauce and lasagne sheets.

  5. Pour over the prepared white sauce and sprinkle with grated parmesan and a little more grated nutmeg. For immediate serving, bake at 190Â°C for 40 mins until golden and bubbly. Sprinkle with basil before serving, if desired.

  6. Once cooled completely, wrap the lasagne dish in cling film and foil. It can be stored for up to 3 months. To serve, thaw for 6 hours in a cool place. Unwrap and bake at 190Â°C for 50-60 mins until thoroughly heated through.
