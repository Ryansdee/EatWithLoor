Ultimate Summer Salad

This post may include affiliate links. Thank you for your support.

Enjoy a refreshing burst of flavors with our Ultimate Summer Salad. This vibrant mix combines crisp Romaine hearts, roasted walnuts, blueberries, raspberries, diced apple, and crumbled feta cheese for a delightful medley of textures and tastes. Pair it with our zesty homemade vinaigrette, crafted from olive oil, lemon juice, sugar, mustard, and honey, allowing you to customize each serving to perfection. Embrace the harmony of sweet and savory in every bite!

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦Serving(s): 4-6

Ingredients

- For the Salad -

🔹 3 Romaine hearts, chopped (or any lettuce)

🔹 150 grams walnuts

🔹 200 grams blueberries

🔹 250 grams raspberries

🔹 1 apple, diced

🔹 200 grams feta cheese, crumbled

- For the Vinaigrette -

🔹 200 milliliters olive oil

🔹 90 milliliters lemon juice

🔹 50 grams granulated sugar

🔹 Pinch of salt

🔹 1 tsp ground mustard

🔹 1 tbsp honey

Instructions

🔹 In a large bowl, toss together the chopped Romaine hearts, roasted       walnuts, blueberries, raspberries, diced apple, and crumbled feta       cheese.

🔹 In a separate bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice,       granulated sugar, salt, and ground mustard until the sugar is       completely dissolved. Taste the dressing and adjust by adding more       lemon juice for desired tartness or more honey to balance acidity.

🔹 Serve the salad dressing on the side, allowing individuals to dress       their salad according to their taste preferences.
