Easy Green Pesto Pasta

This post may include affiliate links. Thank you for your support.

This refreshing blend of cucumber, feta, apple, and green pesto creates a simple yet delightful dish. Whether as a vibrant side or a standalone main, this versatile pasta salad pairs effortlessly with your favorite meat or embraces a vegan twist with diced tofu.

ðŸ•‘Prep Time: 30 minutes

⏰Cook Time: 15 minutes

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦Serving(s): 4

Ingredients

🔹 1 large cucumber

🔹 1 large apple (nectarine is delish too!)

🔹 200 grams of feta cheese

🔹 190 grams green pesto

🔹 Salt and pepper to taste

🔹 250 grams of pasta of choice

Instructions

🔹 Place a pot of water on the heat to boil for the pasta. Cook the       pasta according to the package instructions. Drain, rinse well to       prevent sticking, and let it cool.

🔹 Dice the feta cheese, cucumber and apple into small pieces. In a       bowl, combine the cooled pasta with diced cucumber, feta cheese,       and green pesto.

🔹 Season the pasta salad with salt and pepper, or any other spices, to       taste.

🔹 This pasta salad pairs perfectly with your favorite meat or as a       refreshing side dish for your barbecue.

🔹 For a vegan twist, enhance the protein content by adding diced       cooked tofu to the salad.
