Greek Summer Salad

This post may include affiliate links. Thank you for your support.

Embrace freshness and flavor with our vibrant Greek Summer Salad! Bursting with colorful veggies, tangy olives, and a zesty homemade dressing, this dish offers a delightful medley of textures and tastes. Versatile and refreshing, it's the perfect side or standalone star at any meal.

ðŸ•‘Prep Time: 15 minutes

⏰Cook Time: 25 minutes

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦Serving(s): 1 salad

Ingredients

    - For the Salad -

  • 1 large cucumber, diced

  • A handful of cherry tomatoes, halved

  • 1 green bell pepper, diced

  • 1 red onion, diced

  • A handful of olives, halved

  • 200 grams of feta cheese

    • - For the Vinaigrette -

  • 100 milliliters of olive oil

  • 80 milliliters of balsamic vinegar

  • 1 lemon, juiced

  • 1 tsp of mustard

  • Spices to taste: garlic, oregano, sal, pepper, etc.

Instructions

  1. Place the cucumber, tomatoes, bell pepper, onion, olives, and feta cheese in a large mixing bowl.

  2. In a separate small bowl, combine balsamic vinegar, lemon juice, mustard, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper. Slowly add olive oil, whisking vigorously until the dressing is well mixed.

  3. Drizzle the dressing on the salad and gently stir to combine. Season with additional salt and pepper, if desired.

  4. Enjoy this versatile salad as a perfect side dish for any meal!
