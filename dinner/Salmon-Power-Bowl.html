👨‍👩‍👧‍👦Serving(s): 2

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon filets (about 150-200 grams each)

  • 200 grams white rice (jasmine or basmati)

  • 1 head of broccoli, cut into florets (about 300 grams)

  • 150 grams green beans, trimmed

  • 1 tbsp olive oil

  • Salt and pepper to taste

  • Sesame seeds (optional, for garnish)

  • Soy sauce or teriyaki sauce (optional, for seasoning)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 200Â°C. Meanwhile, cook the rice according to package instructions. Set aside.

  2. Place the 2 salmon filets on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

  3. Drizzle the filets with some olive oil and season them with salt and pepper.

  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the salmon is cooked through and easily flakes with a fork.

  5. Fill a pot with water and bring it to a boil. Add a pinch of salt to the boiling water. Add the broccoli florets and green beans to the boiling water.

  6. Boil for about 4-6 minutes or until the vegetables are tender but still have a slight crunch. Keep an eye on them to avoid overcooking. Once cooked, drain the vegetables.

  7. Divide the cooked rice into bowls.

  8. Add steamed broccoli and green beans on one side of the bowl. Place a cooked salmon filet on the other side. Drizzle with a little soy sauce or teriyaki sauce for added flavor, if desired. Sprinkle sesame seeds on top for garnish.
