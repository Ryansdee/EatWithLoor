Ingredients

🔹 2 salmon filets (about 150-200 grams each)

🔹 200 grams white rice (jasmine or basmati)

🔹 1 head of broccoli, cut into florets (about 300 grams)

🔹 150 grams green beans, trimmed

🔹 1 tbsp olive oil

🔹 Salt and pepper to taste

🔹 Sesame seeds (optional, for garnish)

🔹 Soy sauce or teriyaki sauce (optional, for seasoning)

Instructions

🔹 Preheat your oven to 200Â°C. Meanwhile, cook the rice according to       package instructions. Set aside.

🔹 Place the 2 salmon filets on a baking sheet lined with parchment       paper.

🔹 Drizzle the filets with some olive oil and season them with salt and       pepper.

🔹 Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the salmon is       cooked through and easily flakes with a fork.

🔹 Fill a pot with water and bring it to a boil. Add a pinch of salt to       the boiling water. Add the broccoli florets and green beans to the       boiling water.

🔹 Boil for about 4-6 minutes or until the vegetables are tender but       still have a slight crunch. Keep an eye on them to avoid       overcooking. Once cooked, drain the vegetables.

🔹 Divide the cooked rice into bowls.

🔹 Add steamed broccoli and green beans on one side of the bowl.       Place a cooked salmon filet on the other side. Drizzle with a little       soy sauce or teriyaki sauce for added flavor, if desired. Sprinkle       sesame seeds on top for garnish.
