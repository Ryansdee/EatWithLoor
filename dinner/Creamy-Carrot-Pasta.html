Ingredients

  • 4 Large carrots, peeled and cut into rounds (around 500g)

  • 1 Red onion, peeled and cut into quarters

  • 2 Garlic cloves

  • 2 tbsp olive oil

  • 350 milliliters vegetable or chicken broth

  • 30 milliliters Tomato Paste

  • Spices to taste (e.g., salt, pepper, chili flakes, etc.)

  • 500 grams of pasta of choice

  • Optional: shredded cheese or parmesan to top

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 200Â°C and line a baking tray with baking paper.

  2. Place the carrots, onion, and unpeeled garlic cloves on the baking tray. Drizzle with olive oil and gently toss. Roast for 25-30 minutes.

  3. Remove the skins from the garlic and add them to a blender along with the roasted carrots, onion, tomato paste, and stock. Blend until the sauce is smooth.

  4. Cook the pasta according to instructions, then drain, reserving around 250 milliliters of the pasta water.

  5. If the pasta sauce is too thick, gradually add some of the reserved pasta water to thin it out. Then, add the sauce to the pasta and stir well to coat the pasta evenly.

  6. Serve and add garnish ingredients as desired.
