⏰Cook Time: 15 minutes

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦Serving(s): 4-6

Ingredients

  • 500 grams of pasta of choice

  • 200 grams of canned red kidney beans, drained and rinsed

  • 500 milliliter of tomato sauce

  • 400 grams of canned crushed tomatoes

  • 1 liter water (or any kind of broth for extra taste)

  • 1 red onion, diced

  • 4 garlic cloves, diced

  • A handful of shredded cheese of choice

  • Spices to taste: salt, pepper, garlic, chili powder, etc.

  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil over medium-high heat in a large pot. Add diced onion and cook until softened, stirring occasionally for about 5 minutes. Add garlic and spices, cook for 30 seconds.

  2. Stir in water (or broth), crushed tomatoes, kidney beans, and pasta. Bring the mixture to a simmer.

  3. Simmer until the pasta is cooked to your liking. If it seems too watery, simmer for a few extra minutes to reduce liquid. If too dry, add a splash of water, or broth.

  4. Before serving, stir in the shredded cheese until melted and well combined.

  5. Customize this adaptable pasta dish by adding your preferred spices, beans, or pasta for a personalized meal from your pantry!
