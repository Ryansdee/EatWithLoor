Gimme Green Pasta

This post may include affiliate links. Thank you for your support.

This delightful dish combines creamy avocado and tangy Sriracha with a mix of pasta and zucchini noodles for a flavorful, spicy, and creamy delight! Adjust the spice level and ingredients according to your taste preferences. Enjoy!

ðŸ•‘Prep Time: 15 minutes

⏰Cook Time: 35 minutes

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦Serving(s): 4-6

Ingredients

🔹 250 grams of pasta of choice

🔹 250 grams zucchini noodles

🔹 2 avocado, mashed

🔹 A handful of cherry tomatoes, halved

🔹 500 grams of 0% Fat Greek Yogurt

🔹 5-10 tbsp of Sriracha sauce (depending on how spicy you want      it)

🔹 1 red onion, diced

🔹 4 garlic cloves, diced

🔹 A handful of shredded cheese of choice

🔹 Spices to taste: salt, pepper, garlic, chili powder, etc.

🔹 Olive oil

Instructions

🔹Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add diced onion      and cook until softened, stirring occasionally for about 5      minutes. Add zucchini noodles, garlic, and spices. Cook for an      additional 5 minutes, adjusting cooking time for desired noodle      texture.

🔹 In a separate pot, bring water to a boil and cook the pasta according       to package instructions. Drain and set aside.

🔹In a mixing bowl, combine Greek yogurt, Sriracha sauce, and spices.      Add mashed avocado and halved cherry tomatoes. Mix well with      the cooked pasta.

🔹 In serving bowls, start with a layer of zucchini noodles. Top with      the prepared pasta mixture. Reheat zucchini noodles if necessary.

🔹 Finish by covering the dish with a generous amount of shredded      cheese.
