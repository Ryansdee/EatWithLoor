Red pesto pasta

Experience culinary simplicity with our Red Pesto Pasta. A medley of cherry tomatoes, bell peppers, and red pesto creates a delightful dish. Customize with your favorite meats or add red beans for a vegan twist. This pasta offers a symphony of flavors in every bite.

ðŸ•‘Prep Time: 25 minutes

⏰Cook Time: 15 minutes

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦Serving(s): 4

Ingredients

🔹 250 grams diced cherry tomatoes

🔹 1 red onion, diced

🔹 250 grams diced bell pepper

🔹 190 grams red pesto

🔹 Salt and pepper to taste

🔹 500 grams pasta of your choice

🔹 Butter or oil for cooking

Instructions

🔹 Place a pot of water on the heat to boil for the pasta.

🔹 Dice the cherry tomatoes, bell pepper, and red onion. In a pan over       medium-high heat, add butter or oil. Cook the diced vegetables       until they are soft. Set aside.

🔹 While the vegetables are cooking, cook the pasta according to the       package instructions. Drain the pasta once it's done.

🔹 Mix the cooked pasta with the red pesto, ensuring an even coating.       Add the cooked vegetable mix at the end.

🔹 Season with salt and pepper to taste. Top with cheese if desired.

🔹 This pasta makes a fantastic companion to your favorite meat.       Grilled chicken, shrimp, or sausage can be delightful additions.       Simply cook your preferred meat separately and serve alongside or       mix it into the pasta for a hearty, well-rounded meal

🔹 For a vegan twist, boost the protein by adding red beans to the       dish. Simply mix in cooked red beans with the pasta for a delicious       and plant-based alternative.
