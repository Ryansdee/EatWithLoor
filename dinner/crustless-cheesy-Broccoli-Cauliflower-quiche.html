Crustless Cheesy Broccoli Cauliflower Quiche

This post may include affiliate links. Thank you for your support.

Indulge in our Crustless Cheesy Broccoli Cauliflower Quiche! Packed with vibrant veggies and gooey cheese, it's a delightful twist on classic quiche without the crust. Simple, satisfying, and bursting with flavor, it's a perfect anytime dish.

ðŸ•‘Prep Time: 10 minutes

⏰Cook Time: 45 minutes

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦Serving(s): 6

Ingredients

  • 300 grams broccoli florets

  • 300 grams cauliflower florets

  • Spices of choice (e.g., salt, pepper, garlic, etc.)

  • 6 eggs

  • 6 egg whites

  • 120 grams of 0% Greek yogurt

  • 25 grams chopped spring onions

  • 100 grams shredded cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 175Â°C (350Â°F) and grease a pie plate with butter, or any oil. Set it aside.

  2. Bring a pot of water to a boil. Add the broccoli and cauliflower florets to the boiling water and cook for about six minutes or until they're tender but not overly soft. Then, drain the cooked florets.

  3. Transfer the drained broccoli and cauliflower to a medium-sized bowl. Season them with salt, garlic powder, and black pepper. Gently toss to ensure the spices coat the veggies evenly.

  4. In a separate large bowl, whisk together the eggs, egg whites, and Greek yogurt until well combined.

  5. Place the seasoned vegetables into the prepared pie plate. Sprinkle chopped scallions and shredded cheese over the vegetables. Pour the egg mixture over the cheese, ensuring it covers all the veggies and cheese.

  6. Bake for approximately 45 minutes or until the dish is set, and the top is lightly browned.

  7. Allow it to cool, then slice and serve!
