Quick & Easy Hummus Bowl

Create a delightful and customizable meal with our Hummus Bowl recipe! This easy-to-make dish combines the creamy goodness of hummus with nutritious veggies over a bed of rice, offering a refreshing and satisfying meal. Dive into a flavorful blend of textures and flavors, perfect for a quick lunch or dinner for the whole family

ðŸ•‘Prep Time: 25 minutes

⏰Cook Time: 20 minutes

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦Serving(s): 4-6

Ingredients

🔹 400 grams cooked rice (white or brown)

🔹 400 grams canned chickpea, drained and rinsed

🔹 2 garlic cloves, minced

🔹 3 tbsp lemon juice

🔹 4 tbsp olive oil

🔹 2 cucumbers, diced

🔹 1 red onion, diced

🔹 150 grams of mixed salad greens

🔹 100 grams of olives (green or black)

🔹 Lemon wedges (optional, for garnish)

🔹 Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

🔹 Cook the rice according to package instructions. Set aside once       cooked.

🔹 For the hummus, in a food processor, combine the chickpeas,       minced garlic, lemon juice, olive oil and spices of choice. Blend the       mixture until smooth. If the consistency is too thick, gradually add       a bit of water, a tablespoon at a time, and continue blending until       you reach your desired consistency. Taste and adjust seasonings,       adding more salt or lemon juice if needed.

🔹 Divide the cooked rice among serving bowls. Top each bowl with       generous scoops of homemade hummus.

🔹 Arrange cucumber slices, red onion, mixed salad greens, and olives       around the hummus and rice in each bowl.

🔹 Drizzle a bit of olive oil over the veggies and hummus in each bowl.       Season with salt and pepper to taste. Optionally, serve with lemon       wedges for an extra citrusy touch. Enjoy this delicious meal       alongside warm pita bread or your favorite crackers for a delightful       experience!

🔹 Serve immediately, allowing everyone to mix and enjoy their       customized hummus rice bowls!
