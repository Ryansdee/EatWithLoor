Nice to meet you!

We're Laure (or Loor) and Ryan, siblings and co-founders of Eat With Loor. Our journey began with a love for creating—Laure’s passion for crafting delicious meals and Ryan's knack for building websites.

Simple. Delicious. Yours.

At Eat With Loor, we believe in the power of simple, delightful dishes. Laure's weight loss journey ignited a love for cooking, emphasizing the importance of keeping food enjoyable and uncomplicated. Ryan's expertise in web development complements this vision, ensuring our platform is user-friendly and focused on providing straightforward, trustworthy recipes.

Our goal?

To share budget-friendly recipes that accommodate various dietary needs because great food should be accessible to everyone. No lengthy stories or unnecessary clutter here; just the joy of cooking made easy.
