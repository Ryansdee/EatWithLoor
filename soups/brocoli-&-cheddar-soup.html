Brocoli & Cheddar Soup

This post may include affiliate links. Thank you for your support.

Indulge in the creamy goodness of homemade Cheddar Broccoli Soup. This comforting dish combines tender broccoli florets and carrots in a velvety cheese-infused broth, creating a satisfying and flavorful bowl perfect for any occasion. Follow this simple recipe for a delightful taste of warmth and richness in every spoonful.

ðŸ•‘Prep Time: 10 minutes

⏰Cook Time: 30 minutes

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦Serving(s): 4 - 6

Ingredients

🔹 60 grams unsalted butter (4 tablespoons)

🔹 1 medium sweet onion, chopped

🔹 Spices of choice (e.g., salt, pepper, etc.)

🔹 3 garlic cloves, chopped

🔹 40 grams all-purpose flour

🔹 500 milliliters milk of choice (I used unsweetened almond milk)

🔹 500 milliliters vegetable/chicken broth

🔹 450 grams chopped broccoli florets

🔹 1 large carrot, finely chopped

🔹 250 grams shredded cheddar cheese

🔹 Optional: Homemade croutons, for serving

Instructions

🔹 In a saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter. Add the chopped        onion, salt, pepper and any other spices. Cook until the onion is        softened, for about 5 minutes.

🔹 Add the chopped garlic and cook for an additional minute. Stir in        the all-purpose flour and cook, stirring constantly, for about 2        minutes or until the flour turns golden.

🔹 Gradually pour in the milk, while stirring continuously to avoid        lumps. Pour in the vegetable broth and bring the mixture to a        simmer.

🔹 Add the chopped broccoli florets and carrots. Let it simmer for        about 10-15 minutes until the vegetables are tender.

🔹 Reduce the heat to low and gradually add the shredded cheddar        cheese, stirring until melted and well combined.

🔹 Taste and adjust seasoning with more salt and pepper if needed.

🔹 Serve the soup hot, optionally topped with homemade croutons.        Enjoy your homemade cheesy broccoli soup!
