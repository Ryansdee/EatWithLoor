Thick and Flavorful Sweet Potato Soup

Savor the richness of our Thick and Flavorful Sweet Potato Soup. Packed with zucchini, carrots, and sweet potatoes, this hearty delight offers a naturally thick texture. Adjust the consistency to your liking and enjoy the aromatic blend of thyme, bay leaves, and spices. A comforting bowl that's both wholesome and delicious.

Ingredients

🔹 225 grams of chopped zucchini

🔹 225 grams of chopped carrots

🔹 500 grams of diced sweet potato

🔹 3 liters of water

🔹 Spices: salt, pepper, thym, bay leaves, etc.

Instructions

🔹 Wash and chop the leeks, carrots, and sweet potatoes into         bite-sized pieces.

🔹 In a large pot, combine the chopped zucchini, carrots, diced sweet        potatoes, and 3 liters of water. Adjust the water quantity based on        your preference for thickness. If you prefer a thicker consistency,        use 2 liters; for a slightly thinner consistency, use 3 liters.

🔹 Add salt, pepper, thyme, bay leaves, and any other desired spices to        the pot. Adjust the quantities based on your taste preferences.

🔹 Place the pot over medium-high heat and bring the mixture to a        boil. Once boiling, reduce the heat to a simmer. Allow the        vegetables to cook in the seasoned water until they are soft and        tender, typically around 25-30 minutes.

🔹 Once the vegetables are fully cooked, use an immersion blender or        transfer the soup to a blender to puree until smooth.

🔹 Taste the soup and adjust the seasoning if necessary. Add more        salt, pepper, or spices to suit your taste.

🔹 Ladle the sweet potato soup into bowls. Optionally, garnish with a        drizzle of cream, a sprinkle of fresh shredded cheese, or a dash of        extra spices.
