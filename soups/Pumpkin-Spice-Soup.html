Ingredients

🔹 225 grams of chopped leeks

🔹 225 grams of chopped carrots

🔹 500 grams of diced pumpkin

🔹 2 liters of water

🔹 Spices: salt, pepper, curry, ginger, â€¦

🔹 Optional for thicker consistency: 250 grams zucchini, OR 3 to 4 large potatoes, roughly chopped

Instructions

🔹 Wash and chop the leeks, carrots, and pumpkin into bite-sized       pieces.

🔹 In a large pot, combine the chopped leeks, carrots, diced pumpkin,       and 2 liters of water.

🔹 Add salt, pepper, curry, and ginger to the pot. Adjust the quantities       based on your taste preferences.

🔹 Place the pot over medium-high heat and bring the mixture to a       boil. Once boiling, reduce the heat to a simmer. Allow the       vegetables to cook in the seasoned water until they are soft and       tender, approximately 25-30 minutes.

🔹 Once the vegetables are fully cooked, use an immersion blender or       transfer the soup to a blender to puree until smooth.

🔹 Taste the soup and adjust the seasoning if necessary. Add more       salt, pepper, or spices to suit your taste.

🔹 Ladle the hot pumpkin soup into bowls. Optionally, garnish with a       drizzle of cream, a sprinkle of fresh herbs, or a dash of extra       spices.
