Ingredients

  • 225 grams of chopped leeks

  • 225 grams of chopped carrots

  • 500 grams of diced pumpkin

  • 2 liters of water

  • Spices: salt, pepper, curry, ginger, â€¦

  • Optional for thicker consistency: 250 grams zucchini, OR 3 to 4 large potatoes, roughly chopped

Instructions

  1. Wash and chop the leeks, carrots, and pumpkin into bite-sized pieces.

  2. In a large pot, combine the chopped leeks, carrots, diced pumpkin, and 2 liters of water.

  3. Add salt, pepper, curry, and ginger to the pot. Adjust the quantities based on your taste preferences.

  4. Place the pot over medium-high heat and bring the mixture to a boil. Once boiling, reduce the heat to a simmer. Allow the vegetables to cook in the seasoned water until they are soft and tender, approximately 25-30 minutes.

  5. Once the vegetables are fully cooked, use an immersion blender or transfer the soup to a blender to puree until smooth.

  6. Taste the soup and adjust the seasoning if necessary. Add more salt, pepper, or spices to suit your taste.

  7. Ladle the hot pumpkin soup into bowls. Optionally, garnish with a drizzle of cream, a sprinkle of fresh herbs, or a dash of extra spices.
