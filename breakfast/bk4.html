Grandma's Fluffy Pancakes

This post may include affiliate links. Thank you for your support.

Looking for a comforting breakfast that's a taste of nostalgia? Try Grandma's Fluffy Pancakes, a timeless recipe that will transport you back to your childhood with every bite. These golden, pillowy pancakes are the perfect way to start your day with a smile.

ðŸ•‘Prep Time: 45 minutes

⏰Cook Time: 20 minutes

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦Serving(s): 10 pancakes

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs

  • 170 grams of all-purpose flour

  • 200 milliliters of milk of choice (I used almond milk)

  • 25 grams of butter

  • 20 grams of Stevia sugar (or your preferred sweetener)

  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder

Instructions

  1. Begin by separating the egg whites from the yolks. Place the egg whites in a bowl and beat them until they form stiff peaks. Set this bowl aside in the fridge.

  2. In a separate mixing bowl, combine the sugar with the melted butter. Add the egg yolks and milk to this mixture and stir until well combined.

  3. Gradually add the flour to the mixture, being careful not to create any lumps. Finally, add the baking powder and continue to mix.

  4. Gently fold in the beaten egg whites. Be cautious not to overmix, as this is key to achieving the pancake's fluffiness. Allow the batter to rest in the fridge for at least 30 minutes.

  5. Heat a non-stick pan over medium-high heat. When the pan is hot, pour a portion of the batter to form a pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip the pancake and cook the other side.

  6. Serve your pancakes with your favorite toppings and enjoy!
