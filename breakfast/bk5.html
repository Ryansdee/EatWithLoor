Easy Protein Banana Pancakes

Savor a guilt-free breakfast with our Easy Protein Banana Pancakes‚Äîcombining the goodness of oat flour, protein powder, and ripe bananas. Quick to make and customizable with your favorite toppings, these pancakes are a delicious and nutritious way to kickstart your day!

üïëPrep Time: 25 minutes

⏰Cook Time: 20 minutes

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦Serving(s): 12 pancakes

Ingredients

  • 40 grams of oat flour

  • 50 grams of protein powder of choice (I used MyProtein Impact Whey Protein Chocolate Brownie)

  • 100 grams of egg whites

  • 3 tablespoons of baking powder

  • 50 milliliters of milk of choice (I used almond milk)

  • A couple drops of vanilla extract

  • 1,5 ripe banana

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix oat flour, protein powder, and baking powder together.

  2. Stir in mashed bananas, egg whites, milk, and vanilla extract into the dry mixture. Mix until well combined.

  3. Heat a non-stick pan over medium-high heat. Optionally, add a small amount of butter or oil to the pan.

  4. When the pan is hot, pour a portion of the batter to form a pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip the pancake and cook the other side.

  5. Serve your pancakes with your favorite toppings and enjoy!
