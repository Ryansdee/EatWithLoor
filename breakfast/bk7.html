Red Berry Smoothie Bowl

This post may include affiliate links. Thank you for your support.

Start your day with a burst of energy by whipping up our simple and delicious Red Berry Smoothie Bowl! This vibrant bowl is packed with the natural sweetness of red berries and is a refreshing, nutritious breakfast option that takes just minutes to prepare.

ðŸ•‘Prep Time: 5 minutes

⏰Cook Time: 0 minutes

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦Serving(s): 1 bowl

Ingredients

  • 1,5 bananas

  • 250 grams of frozen fruit of choice (I used red berry mix)

  • 180 milliliters of choice of milk (I used almond milk)

  • Any toppings of your choice: strawberries, coconut flakes, etc.

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine the bananas, frozen berries, and milk. It's crucial that the fruit is still fully frozen for a satisfying texture.

  2. Blend until smooth and velvety.

  3. Pour into a bowl and add your favorite toppings, such as fresh strawberries, banana slices or coconut flakes.

  4. Enjoy your quick, delightful, and purely fruity Red Berry Smoothie Bowl!
