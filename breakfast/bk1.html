Protein Powder Oatmeal

This post may include affiliate links. Thank you for your support.

Seeking to meet your daily protein target with ease and satisfaction? You're in luck, because I've got a delectable solution that will not only elevate the protein content of your morning oatmeal but also keep you feeling full and energized until the lunchtime bell chimes. This effortless culinary upgrade will make your oatmeal a protein-packed powerhouse, delivering a delightful and satiating start to your day.

üïëPrep Time: 5 minutes

⏰Cook Time: 10 minutes

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦Serving(s): 1 bowl

Ingredients

  • 50 grams of rolled oats

  • 165 ml of water (or milk of choice)

  • 25 grams of protein powder of choice (I used MyProtein Impact Whey Protein Chocolate Brownie)

  • Your preferred oatmeal toppings (e.g., sliced bananas, strawberries, blueberries, chocolate chips)

Instructions

  1. Place a pot on the stove and add the water. Heat it over medium heat until the water begins to boil.

  2. Gently add the rolled oats while stirring. Keep stirring until the oats have nearly soaked up the water. Lower the heat if needed.

  3. Remove the pot from the heat source and blend in the protein powder. If the oatmeal becomes too thick for your preference, feel free to add a bit more water or milk to reach your desired consistency.

  4. Transfer the oatmeal to a bowl and add your favorite oatmeal toppings. Enjoy!
