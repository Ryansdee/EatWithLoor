Loaded Egg Muffins

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦Serving(s): 12 Muffins

Ingredients

  • 8 eggs

  • 120 milliliters milk (I used soya milk)

  • 120 grams shredded cheese (cheddar works fine)

  • 120 grams diced bell peppers (mixed colors)

  • 60 grams baby spinach, roughly chopped

  • Spices of choice: salt, pepper, paprika, etc.

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 180Â°C. Grease a nonstick muffin pan.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the eggs and milk until well combined.

  3. Stir in the shredded cheddar cheese, diced bell peppers, chopped baby spinach, salt, and any additional omelet ingredients you prefer. If you are using any meat, make sure the meat is already precooked before adding it to the muffin batter.

  4. Scoop the batter into the muffin tin, filling each cup almost to the rim. This should yield about 12 muffins.

  5. Bake for approximately 20-25 minutes or until the eggs are cooked. The muffins will puff up initially but will settle as they cool.

  6. Allow the egg muffins to cool in the tin for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack. Serve these muffins warm or at room temperature for a delicious and protein-packed breakfast option.
