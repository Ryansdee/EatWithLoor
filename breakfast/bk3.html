Chocolate Banana Baked Oats

This post may include affiliate links. Thank you for your support.

Are you on a mission to meet your daily protein target? Look no further, because I've got just the solution for you. Brace yourself for a scrumptious and hassle-free method to elevate the protein quotient in your oatmeal without resorting to protein powder. Whether you're an avid fitness enthusiast, a health-conscious individual, or simply someone who appreciates a nutritious breakfast, this ingenious hack will make your morning meal not only satisfying but also incredibly nutritious. Say goodbye to mundane, flavorless oats, and say hello to a whole new world of protein-packed delight!

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦Serving(s): 4 ramekins

Ingredients

  • 100 grams of rolled oats

  • 1 banana

  • 1 tbsp of honey (or any sweetener of choice)

  • 2 eggs

  • 100 grams of chocolate chips

  • 1 tbsp baking powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180Â°C.

  2. In a blender or food processor, pulse the rolled oats until they turn into a flour-like consistency.

  3. Add baking powder, sliced banana, honey, and eggs to the blender. Blend until you achieve a smooth mixture. Gently stir in the chocolate chips.

  4. Lightly grease four oven-safe ramekins with oil or butter. Evenly divide the batter among the ramekins. Optionally, top with additional oats or add a piece of chocolate in the middle for extra indulgence.

  5. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes, or until the baked oats have risen and spring back when lightly pressed.

  6. Customize your baked oats with toppings like blueberries, banana slices, melted chocolate, or any other favorites.

  7. Enjoy your delightful chocolate banana baked oats!
