Greek Yogurt Oatmeal

This post may include affiliate links. Thank you for your support.

Are you on a mission to meet your daily protein target? Look no further, because I've got just the solution for you. Brace yourself for a scrumptious and hassle-free method to elevate the protein quotient in your oatmeal without resorting to protein powder. Whether you're an avid fitness enthusiast, a health-conscious individual, or simply someone who appreciates a nutritious breakfast, this ingenious hack will make your morning meal not only satisfying but also incredibly nutritious. Say goodbye to mundane, flavorless oats, and say hello to a whole new world of protein-packed delight!

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦Serving(s): 1 bowl

Ingredients

  • 50 grams of rolled oats

  • 165 ml of water (or milk of choice)

  • 30 grams of 0% Fat Greek yogurt

  • Your preferred oatmeal toppings (e.g., sliced bananas, strawberries, blueberries, chocolate chips)

Instructions

  1. Place a pot on the stove and add the water. Heat it over medium heat until the water begins to boil.

  2. Gently add the rolled oats while stirring. Keep stirring until the oats have nearly soaked up the water. Lower the heat if needed.

  3. Remove the pot from the heat source and blend in the Greek yogurt. If the oatmeal becomes too thick for your preference, feel free to add a bit more water or milk to reach your desired consistency.

  4. Transfer the oatmeal to a bowl and add your favorite oatmeal toppings. Enjoy!
