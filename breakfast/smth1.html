Easy Strawberry-Banana Smoothie

This post may include affiliate links. Thank you for your support.

Experience a delightful fusion of strawberries and bananas in this creamy, refreshing smoothie. Each sip embodies the natural sweetness of ripe fruits, promising a burst of deliciousness in every glass.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦Serving(s): 2

Ingredients

  • 250 milliliters of milk choice (I used almond milk)

  • 150 grams of Strawberry greek yogurt

  • 300 grams of fresh or frozen strawberries (or any other red fruit)

  • 1 (frozen) banana

  • Optional: honey for sweetness

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine the milk, Greek yogurt, frozen banana, and strawberries.

  2. Blend until smooth. Add a splash more milk or water if the mixture is too thick to blend easily.

  3. Taste the smoothie and, if desired, add a bit of honey for extra sweetness.

  4. Pour the smoothie into two glasses. Add ice cubes if preferred.

  5. Savor your quick, delightful strawberry-banana smoothie!
