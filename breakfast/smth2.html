Berry Burst Protein Power Smoothie

This post may include affiliate links. Thank you for your support.

Get ready to power up your day with a delightful blend of fruits and protein in our 'Berry Burst Protein Power Smoothie.' Packed with vibrant flavors of blueberries, banana, and a touch of spinach for added nutrition, this quick and easy-to-make smoothie is a delicious way to fuel your body and start your day on a healthy note.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦Serving(s): 2

Ingredients

  • 300 milliliters of milk choice (I used almond milk)

  • 1 scoop of protein powder of your choice (I used Vanilla flavor from MyProtein)

  • 30 grams of oatmeal

  • 1 (frozen) banana

  • 100 grams of (frozen) blueberries

  • Optional: handful of spinach leaves (you won't taste it, I promise!)

Instructions

  1. Combine the almond milk, protein powder, oatmeal, frozen banana, frozen blueberries, and spinach leaves in a blender.

  2. Blend until smooth. If it's too thick, add a little more milk or water to help with blending.

  3. Pour the smoothie into two glasses. Add ice cubes if desired.

  4. Enjoy your quick, delicious, and protein-rich smoothie!
