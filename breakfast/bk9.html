Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread

  • 1 ripe avocado

  • Pomegranate seeds

  • 50 grams of soft goat cheese

  • Spices of choice: paprika, salt, pepper, etc.

  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread slices to your desired level of crispiness.

  2. While the bread is toasting, peel and pit the ripe avocado. Mash the avocado in a bowl with a fork until smooth. Add a pinch of salt and pepper to taste.

  3. Spread the mashed avocado evenly over each toasted bread slice. Sprinkle pomegranate seeds generously over the avocado layer. Crumble the soft goat cheese on top of the pomegranate seeds.
