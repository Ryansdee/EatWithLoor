Eat-The-Rainbow Chicken salad

This post may include affiliate links. Thank you for your support.

Experience a burst of freshness and flavor with our Eat-The-Rainbow Chicken Salad! This vibrant medley of crisp spinach, grated carrot, red cabbage, and iceberg lettuce, topped with tender chicken slices and dressed in zesty balsamic vinaigrette, offers a deliciously wholesome meal. Embrace this colorful feast, packed with diverse textures and nutrients, for a delightful and nutritious dining experience!

Ingredients

  • 50 grams of fresh spinach

  • 50 grams of grated carrot

  • 50 grams of finely chopped red cabbage

  • 50 grams of iceberg salad

  • 50 grapes of chopped cherry tomatoes

  • 30 grams of diced red onion

  • 30 grams of corn

  • Â½ avocado sliced

  • 200 grams of chicken breast

    • - For the Vinaigrette -

  • 15 milliliters balsamic vinegar

  • 30 milliliters olive oil

  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare Vegetables: Wash and peel a small carrot. Grate it using a grater and set aside. Finely chop 50 grams of red cabbage and set aside. Wash and tear 50 grams of iceberg lettuce into bite-sized pieces. Pat dry and set aside. Peel and dice 30 grams of red onion. Set aside. Cut a ripe avocado in half, remove the pit, and slice Â½ of it. Set aside. Cut the cherry tomatoes into halves and set aside. Keep the corn ready.

  2. Prepare chicken: Season the chicken breast with salt and pepper or your preferred spices. Heat a pan over medium-high heat with a drizzle of olive oil. Cook the chicken breast for about 6-7 minutes per side or until fully cooked through. Allow it to rest for a few minutes, then slice it into strips.

  3. In a large bowl, combine the prepared fresh spinach, grated carrot, chopped red cabbage, torn iceberg lettuce, diced red onion, sliced avocado, and cooked chicken breast.

  4. In a small bowl, whisk together balsamic vinegar and olive oil. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Drizzle the prepared vinaigrette over the salad ingredients and toss gently to coat everything evenly.

  5. Plate the salad and serve immediately for a wholesome and nutritious meal.
