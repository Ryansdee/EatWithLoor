Instructions

  1. Boil salted water and cook pasta until al dente according to package directions. Drain and let it cool.

  2. In a large bowl, mix the cooled pasta, drained tuna, finely chopped red onion, halved cherry tomatoes, and diced cucumber. Feel free to add other ingredients you like (e.g., feta cheese, apple, fried onion, etc.)!

  3. Make Vinaigrette: In a small bowl, whisk together balsamic vinegar and olive oil. Season with salt and pepper according to taste.

  4. Drizzle the prepared vinaigrette over the salad and toss gently to coat all ingredients evenly.

  5. Enjoy the tuna pasta salad immediately or refrigerate for a chilled dish.
