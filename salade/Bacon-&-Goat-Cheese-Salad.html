Bacon & Goat Cheese Salad

This post may include affiliate links. Thank you for your support.

Discover a delightful Bacon & Goat Cheese Salad adorned with homemade croutons! This vibrant dish features baked goat cheese, crispy bacon, homemade croutons and shredded carrots atop fresh lettuce. Accompanied by crunchy walnuts and a drizzle of honey, it's served with a zesty vinaigrette of lemon, mayo, and olive oil. Quick and satisfying, this salad is a symphony of flavors and textures that's both effortless and delicious.

Ingredients

- For the Salad -

🔹 250 grams of goat cheese

🔹 150 grams of bacon, sliced

🔹 2 avocados

🔹 150 grams of lettuce of choice

🔹 50 grams of shredded carrots

🔹 A handful of walnuts

🔹 A handful of croutons

🔹 Optional: honey

- For the Vinaigrette -

🔹 1 tbsp lemon juice

🔹 120 milliliters of mayonnaise

🔹 50 milliliters of olive oil

🔹 Salt and pepper
