Bacon & Goat Cheese Salad

This post may include affiliate links. Thank you for your support.

Discover a delightful Bacon & Goat Cheese Salad adorned with homemade croutons! This vibrant dish features baked goat cheese, crispy bacon, homemade croutons and shredded carrots atop fresh lettuce. Accompanied by crunchy walnuts and a drizzle of honey, it's served with a zesty vinaigrette of lemon, mayo, and olive oil. Quick and satisfying, this salad is a symphony of flavors and textures that's both effortless and delicious.

Ingredients

    - For the Salad -

  • 250 grams of goat cheese

  • 150 grams of bacon, sliced

  • 2 avocados

  • 150 grams of lettuce of choice

  • 50 grams of shredded carrots

  • A handful of walnuts

  • A handful of croutons

  • Optional: honey

    • - For the Vinaigrette -

  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

  • 120 milliliters of mayonnaise

  • 50 milliliters of olive oil

  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 200Â°C. Line a baking dish with parchment paper. Line a baking dish with parchment paper. Slice the goat cheese into rounds and place them on the parchment paper. Bake on the upper rack for 15-20 minutes until golden.

  2. For homemade croutons, slice leftover bread, drizzle it with olive oil and spices, and bake it alongside the goat cheese. Keep an eye on them, as they might need to be taken out earlier depending on your oven.

  3. Meanwhile, in a large non-stick pan over medium heat, fry the bacon until crispy, turning occasionally.

  4. In a bowl, mix together the lemon juice, mayonnaise, olive oil, salt, and pepper until well combined.

  5. Arrange the avocado slices, shredded carrots, crispy bacon, homemade croutons, and baked goat cheese rounds on top of the lettuce. Optionally, drizzle a little honey over the goat cheese. Sprinkle the walnuts on top.

  6. Serve the salad with the prepared vinaigrette.
