Spicy Spinach Pasta Salad on the Go

Spice up your salad game with our Spicy Pasta Salad! This dish combines al dente pasta, fresh veggies, creamy feta, and baby spinach, all tossed in a Sriracha yogurt dressing. It's a flavorful, satisfying meal that brings a kick to your taste buds!

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, bring salted water to a boil. Cook pasta until al dente according to package directions. Drain and let it cool.

  2. In a large bowl, combine cooled pasta, grape tomatoes, chopped cucumber, diced red onion, crumbled feta cheese and baby spinach.. Toss to mix everything. Set aside.

  3. In a small bowl, whisk together yogurt, sriracha sauce and spices. Adjust Sriracha or spices for more heat if desired.

  4. When ready to serve, divide the salad into two bowls. Drizzle with the prepared vinaigrette and toss to coat evenly. Alternatively, toss the entire salad in a single bowl and serve.
