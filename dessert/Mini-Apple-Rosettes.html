Mini Apple Rosettes

This post may include affiliate links. Thank you for your support.

Indulge in a delightful treat with our Mini Apple Rosettes! This fuss-free recipe features thinly sliced apples wrapped in flaky puff pastry, creating charming rosettes bursting with sweet apple goodness. Perfectly portioned and effortlessly baked to golden perfection, these treats are a delightful addition to any occasion, whether as a dessert or a delightful snack.

Ingredients

🔹2 sheets of puff pastry

🔹3 apples, thinly sliced

🔹3 tbsp lemon juice

🔹Sweetener of choice (e.g., jam, honey, etc.)

🔹3 tbsp white sugar

Instructions

🔹Preheat your oven to 180Â°C. Grease and flour a cake mold.

🔹Thinly slice all the apples. In a bowl, toss the apple slices with      lemon juice and sugar until coated. Microwave for 5 minutes until      slightly tender. Pat dry with paper towels.

🔹Roll out the puff pastry sheets and cut each into 6 equal slices.      Spoon a bit of sweetener onto each pastry slice.

🔹Place apple slices along the pastry with the peel side out. Roll from      one end to the other, creating a rosette shape. Place each rosette in      a muffin tin or muffin paper liner.

🔹Bake the rosettes in the preheated oven for 35 to 40 minutes until      they turn golden brown.

🔹Once baked, remove the apple rosettes from the oven and let them      cool for a few minutes in the muffin tin. Carefully transfer them to a      wire rack to cool completely.

🔹Serve the apple rosettes warm or at room temperature. They can be      enjoyed as a delightful dessert or snack. Optionally, dust with      powdered sugar for a decorative touch before serving.
