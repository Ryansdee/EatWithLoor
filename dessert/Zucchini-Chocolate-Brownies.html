Ingredients

  • 4 eggs

  • 80 grams of all purpose flour

  • 100 grams of brown sugarr

  • 200 grams of shredded zucchini

  • 200 grams dark chocolate

  • 150 grams of butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 180Â°C. Grease and flour a brownie cake mold.

  2. Break the chocolate into pieces and melt it using your preferred method: microwave or using a double boiler (au bain marie). Mix in the butter until smooth.

  3. Separate the egg whites and yolks into two bowls. In the bowl with the egg yolks, add the sugar and whisk until you get a fluffy mixture.

  4. Gradually add the flour and the melted chocolate into the egg yolk mixture, stirring until well combined.

  5. Stir in the shredded zucchini until fully incorporated.

  6. In the other bowl, whip the egg whites until stiff peaks form. Gently fold the whipped egg whites into the batter until just combined.

  7. If the batter is too liquid (zucchini can be watery): gradually add small amounts of flour, a tablespoon at a time, and mix until you achieve a thicker consistency. If the batter is too dry, incorporate additional liquid ingredients gradually, such as a bit more melted butter or oil, a small amount of milk, or even a tablespoon of yogurt to moisten the batter without changing the flavor significantly.

  8. Pour the batter into a prepared cake pan and bake for approximately 25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
