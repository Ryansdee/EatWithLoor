Ingredients

🔹4 eggs

🔹80 grams of all purpose flour

🔹100 grams of brown sugarr

🔹200 grams of shredded zucchini

🔹200 grams dark chocolate

🔹150 grams of butter

Instructions

🔹 Preheat your oven to 180Â°C. Grease and flour a brownie cake mold.

🔹 Break the chocolate into pieces and melt it using your preferred       method: microwave or using a double boiler (au bain marie). Mix in       the butter until smooth.

🔹 Separate the egg whites and yolks into two bowls. In the bowl with       the egg yolks, add the sugar and whisk until you get a fluffy       mixture.

🔹 Gradually add the flour and the melted chocolate into the egg yolk       mixture, stirring until well combined.

🔹 Stir in the shredded zucchini until fully incorporated.

🔹 In the other bowl, whip the egg whites until stiff peaks form.       Gently fold the whipped egg whites into the batter until just       combined.

🔹If the batter is too liquid (zucchini can be watery): gradually add       small amounts of flour, a tablespoon at a time, and mix until you       achieve a thicker consistency. If the batter is too dry, incorporate       additional liquid ingredients gradually, such as a bit more melted       butter or oil, a small amount of milk, or even a tablespoon of       yogurt to moisten the batter without changing the flavor       significantly.

🔹Pour the batter into a prepared cake pan and bake for approximately       25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out       clean.
