Chocolate Chip Bread

This simple yet scrumptious treat is a perfect blend of zesty lemons, creating a moist and flavorful loaf that's a breeze to make. With a handful of basic ingredients and a dash of lemony freshness, this recipe promises a quick and delightful baking experience. Slice into a world of citrus-infused goodness with our Easy Peasy Lemon Bread ‚Äì a treat that's as easy to bake as it is to enjoy!

⏰Cook Time: 45 minutes

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦Serving(s): 8-10 slices

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs

  • 250 grams of all-purpose flour

  • 1 tbsp baking powder

  • 80 grams butter, softened

  • 150 grams white granulated sugar

  • 240 milliliter milk

  • 150 grams chocolate chips

  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 170¬∞C. Grease and flour a loaf pan or line it with parchment paper.

  2. In a bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.

  3. Using a mixer, beat the softened butter and sugar in a separate bowl until light and fluffy. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.

  4. Add half of the dry mixture to the wet mixture and stir until just combined. Add half of the milk and stir until just combined. Repeat with the remaining dry ingredients and milk. Avoid over-mixing; ensure the dry and wet ingredients are just combined. Fold in the chocolate chips.

  5. Pour the batter in the pan. Bake for approximately 45-50 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out almost clean. Remove from the oven and allow it to cool on a wire cooling rack.

  6. Once cooled, slice the chocolate chip bread and enjoy the delicious homemade treat!
