Homemade Banana Bread

This post may include affiliate links. Thank you for your support.

This simple yet irresistible recipe boasts the perfect blend of overripe bananas, wholesome flour, and a touch of brown sugar, baked to golden perfection. Each moist and tender slice promises a burst of sweet banana flavor, making it an ideal treat for breakfast, a snack, or any time you crave a cozy, homemade delight.

⏰Cook Time: 60 minutes

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦Serving(s): 8-10 slices

Ingredients

🔹2 eggs

🔹2 overripe bananas

🔹1 tbsp baking powder

🔹60 grams of butter

🔹250 grams of flour

🔹95 grams of brown sugar

Instructions

🔹Preheat your oven to 180Â°C. Grease and flour a loaf pan or line it      with parchment paper.

🔹In a mixing bowl, mash the overripe bananas until they form a      smooth consistency.

🔹In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Melt the butter and let it cool      slightly. Add the beaten eggs and melted butter to the mashed      bananas. Mix well.

🔹In another bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, and brown      sugar. Combine the wet banana mixture with the dry ingredients.      Stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps are      fine.

🔹Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan. Bake in the preheated      oven for about 50-60 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the      center comes out clean. You can add slices of bananas, chocolate      chips or nuts to decorate.

🔹Once baked, remove the banana bread from the oven and let it cool      in the pan for 10-15 minutes.

🔹Transfer the bread to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing.

🔹Enjoy your homemade banana bread!
