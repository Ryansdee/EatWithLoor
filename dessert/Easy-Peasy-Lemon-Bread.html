Ingredients

  • 2 eggs

  • 125 grams of butter

  • 200 grams of white granulated sugar

  • 1 tbsp grated lemon zest (approximately from 2-3 lemons)

  • 4 tbsp lemon juice (approximately from 4-5 lemons)

  • 1 tbsp baking powder

  • 125 milliliters of milk of choice (I used unsweetened almond milk)

  • 60 grams of powdered sugar

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 180Â°C. Grease and flour a loaf pan or line it with parchment paper.

  2. In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the eggs, lemon zest, and 2 tbsp of lemon juice until well combined.

  3. In another bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, and salt. Gradually stir the dry mixture into the creamed mixture, alternating with the milk. Beat well after each addition.

  4. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan. Bake for 40-45 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.

  5. Allow the bread to cool in the pan for 15 minutes before removing it to a wire rack.

  6. In a small bowl, mix together the powdered sugar and 2 tbsp of lemon juice to create the glaze.

  7. When the bread is cooled, drizzle the glaze over the top. Be sure the cake is cooled completely before applying the glaze. If the cake is too warm the glaze may run off the cake.
