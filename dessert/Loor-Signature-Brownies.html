Discover the essence of indulgence with Loor's Signature Chocolate Brownies. These timeless treats embody the pure pleasure of rich, moist chocolatey goodness. Loved for their simplicity and irresistible flavor, these classic brownies are Loor's hallmark, promising pure satisfaction in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 200 grams of chocolate of choice (I used dark)

  • 4 eggs

  • 50 grams of granulated sugar

  • 50 grams of brown sugar

  • 2 tsp vanilla sugar

  • 80 grams of all purpose flour

  • 150 grams of butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 180Â°C. Grease and flour a brownie cake mold.

  2. Break the chocolate into pieces and melt it using your preferred method: microwave or using a double boiler (au bain marie). Mix in the butter until smooth.

  3. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract until well combined. Gradually add the flour into the egg mixture, stirring until fully incorporated.

  4. Pour in the melted chocolate mixture and mix thoroughly until you achieve a smooth, homogeneous batter.

  5. Pour the batter into the prepared brownie cake mold.

  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes in the preheated oven, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  7. Allow the brownie cake to cool before slicing. Serve as is or with a scoop of ice cream. You can also dust the top with powdered sugar for an extra touch of sweetness.
