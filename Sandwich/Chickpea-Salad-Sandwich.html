Chickpea Salad Sandwich

This post may include affiliate links. Thank you for your support.

Indulge in these wholesome Chickpea Salad Sandwiches, a delightful and simple meal featuring seasoned mashed chickpeas, tangy lemon, and zesty mustard combined for a flavorful filling. Layered with crisp lettuce, diced red onion, and fresh tomato slices, these sandwiches offer a burst of vibrant flavors between toasted bread slices, perfect for a quick and satisfying meal.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦Serving(s): 4 sandwiches

Ingredients

  • 500 grams of rinsed chickpea

  • 50 milliliters of olive oil

  • 25 milliliters of lemon juice

  • 2 tsp mustard

  • Spices of choice: salt, pepper, garlic, etc

  • 8 slices of bread, toasted

  • Handful of lettuce

  • Â½ red onion, diced

  • 1 tomato, sliced

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine the rinsed chickpeas, olive oil, lemon juice, mustard, and desired spices (like salt, pepper, garlic, etc.). Mash or crush the chickpeas using a fork or potato masher until they reach your preferred consistency. Adjust seasoning to taste.

  2. Arrange the toasted bread slices on a clean surface. Spread the seasoned chickpea mixture evenly over 4 slices of the toasted bread.

  3. Top each slice with lettuce, diced red onion, and sliced tomato. Place the remaining 4 slices of toasted bread on top to form sandwiches.

  4. Enjoy your delightful and easy-to-make Chickpea Salad Sandwiches! Adjust the seasonings and toppings as per your preference.
