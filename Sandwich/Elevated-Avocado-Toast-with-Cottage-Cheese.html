Instructions

  1. Toast 8 slices of bread until golden brown.

  2. Spoon 300 grams of cottage cheese onto the slices, distributing it evenly.

  3. Mash 1 whole avocado and spread it evenly over the toasted bread slices.

  4. Layer sliced tomato and cucumber on top of the avocado.

  5. Season with spices of your choice, such as salt, pepper, or paprika, as desired.
